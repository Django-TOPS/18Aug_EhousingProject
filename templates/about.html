SUN SIMPOLO

Sun Simpolo is a project by Sun Builders in Ahmedabad,It is a Ready to Move project. Sun Simpolo offers some of the most conveniently designed Apartment Located in Bopal, it is a residential project It has 224 units, There are 7 buildings in this project Sun Simpolo offers some of the most exclusive 1 BHK, 1.5 BHK. As per the area plan, units are in the size range of 585.0 - 720.0 sq.feet Launched in June 2015, Sun Simpolo is slated for possession in Jun 2017. The address of Sun Simpolo is Opp. Samprat Residency, Besides Neelkanth Villa, Bopal Shilaj Road, Bopal.

Sun Simpolo ensures a coveted lifestyle and offers a convenient living. It offers facilities such as Lift, Power Backup. For families with kids, there is Children's Play Area, nearby apart from Indoor Game Room - Billiards/Snooker Table, Carom Board, Table Tennis, Foosball Table, Sports Area. There is 24x7 Security.

Sun Builders is a reputed developer firm established in 1981. The firm has since then developed 40 projects and focuses on customer-centricity. Bopal is well-connected to other parts of city by road, which passes through the heart of this suburb. Prominent shopping malls, movie theatres, school, and hospitals are present in proximity of this residential project.
